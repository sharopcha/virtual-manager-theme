What is virtual manager?

Virtual Manager is an online football manager game, where you take the reigns as manager of your own football club. You will buy and sell players, setup amazing tactics, train your team, manage the finances of the club, and much more.

As you strive for the gold and advance up the leagues, you will gain friends and rivals among the thousands of other players. Make the right decisions and you may become the greatest club in the world of Virtual Manager.
